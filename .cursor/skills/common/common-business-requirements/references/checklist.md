# BRD Checklist

- [ ] Business objective is measurable (baseline + target + date).
- [ ] Executive summary names purpose, desired outcome, sponsor, and validation owner.
- [ ] Sponsor and decision owners identified.
- [ ] AS-IS pain and TO-BE outcomes documented.
- [ ] Complex process has Mermaid AS-IS/TO-BE diagram or explicit "not needed" note.
- [ ] Scope and non-scope explicitly listed.
- [ ] Assumptions and constraints listed.
- [ ] Risk register includes mitigation owner.
- [ ] Value statement is tied to cost, revenue, risk, compliance, or cycle time.
- [ ] Cost-benefit or tradeoff is explicit enough for approval.
- [ ] Domain terms that can be misread appear in the glossary.
- [ ] Handoff links to candidate PRD requirements exist.
- [ ] Functional behavior is routed to PRD/SRS instead of BRD.
